## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Show Linh real-time revenue, room utilization, and bill history for today's shift. At shift end, verify everything balances and generate the end-of-day report. This is Linh's peace-of-mind page.

**User Situation:** After closing a bill, Linh lands here to confirm the numbers updated. At midnight when the last customer leaves, she reviews everything, generates the report, and logs out knowing the till matches the system.

**Success Criteria:** Key metrics visible at a glance (no scrolling needed). All closed bills listed and searchable. End-of-day report generates in one click. Till vs. system discrepancy is obvious and flagged.

**Entry Points:**
- After confirming payment (01.5)
- From main nav "Dashboard"
- From any page via sidebar

**Exit Points:**
- Nav → Room Status Board, Menu, or Rooms
- End of shift → Logout

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Default | After bill close or nav click | All KPIs loaded, bills table populated | Browse, search, generate report |
| Empty (no bills) | Start of shift, nothing closed yet | KPIs show 0. Table shows "No bills yet today" / "Chưa có hóa đơn hôm nay" | Wait for activity |
| Loading | Data fetching | Skeleton placeholders on cards and table | Wait |
| Discrepancy | Till cash ≠ system total (manual entry vs. auto) | Red alert banner at top with amount difference | Review bills, adjust till entry |

---

## Technical Notes

- All data scoped to current venue (from login context) and current day
- KPI cards auto-refresh on WebSocket events (bill close, check-in, checkout)
- End of Day Report: printable HTML page or PDF download. Includes venue name, date, shift summary
- Historical data: date picker to view past days (read-only)
- Discrepancy alert: if user enters "till cash" and it doesn't match system total, flag it
