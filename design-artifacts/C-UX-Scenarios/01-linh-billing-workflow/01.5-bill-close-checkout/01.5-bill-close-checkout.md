## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Present the final auto-calculated bill, allow a last discount or adjustment, record payment, and close the session. The moment of truth — this bill must be perfectly accurate and presentable to the customer.

**User Situation:** Room 302's session is ending. The customer is at the counter ready to pay. Linh needs to show them the bill, accept payment, and close the room — all in under 30 seconds.

**Success Criteria:** Bill reflects every item and the exact room duration. Payment processed in one click. Room status changes to Cleaning immediately. Receipt prints automatically.

**Entry Points:**
- "Close Bill" from Active Room Session (01.4)
- "Generate Bill" from Room Status Board (01.2)

**Exit Points:**
- Confirm payment → Revenue Dashboard (01.6)
- Back → Active Room Session (01.4) if customer wants to add more

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Default | Bill loaded | Full bill displayed with all items and auto-calculated totals | Review, apply discount, process payment |
| Cash payment | Cash method selected | Amount Tendered + Change Due fields visible | Enter amount, confirm |
| Transfer/Card | Non-cash method | No change calculation. Direct confirm | Confirm payment |
| Insufficient amount | Cash tendered < grand total | Change Due red, negative. Confirm button disabled | Correct amount |
| Processing | After Confirm clicked | Button shows spinner. All fields disabled | Wait |
| Success | Payment confirmed | Flash to Revenue Dashboard. Receipt prints | Done |

## Technical Notes

- Bill number auto-generated (YYYYMMDD-NNNN format, sequential per day)
- Session timer stops server-side on Close Bill action — prevents manipulation
- Receipt auto-print via browser window.print() or thermal printer API
- Payment is final — no undo. Room status changes to Cleaning irreversibly
- Digital record stored: bill ID, all items, room charge, discount, payment method, amount, timestamp, cashier
